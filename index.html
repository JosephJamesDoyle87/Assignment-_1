Crisis in Aleppo

Since the conflict in Syria began, more than 250,000 people have died, 7.6 million people are displaced within Syria, and 4.6 million have fled to neighbouring countries. Millions have been left in need of urgent humanitarian assistance. Oxfam is on the ground in Syria, as well as in Syria's neighbouring countries Lebanon, Jordan and Turkey. In Europe, our programmes are in Greece, Italy, Serbia and Macedonia.In Syria, Jordan and Lebanon, we have reached over 1.6 million people with life-saving clean water, sanitation and vital support for families who have lost everything. Our teams are rapidly assessing further need. Help us to do more in Syria and beyond.
